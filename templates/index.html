This is the Fusion Byte of the Data Pipeline class. This page should explain what question you want to answer and use the visualization tools provided by Google Fusion Table to answer it.

Question: Which states have the largest density of animals in USA.

Using the sort function in google fusion table, we can sort all states according to 'Estimated animal manure per farm land area (kg of N/km2)', shown on the fourth column. Our result bases on the assumption the fact that density of animal manure can represent the density of animals. According to sorting result, top 5 states are: Delaware, North Carolina, Maryland, Pennsylvania and Georgia. The heapmap below the data is a good visualization for animal distribution. As color goes from green to yellow to red, density of animals increases. It is easily to see that there are more animals on east coast than on middle west coast of USA. Most top states in the table above are on the east coast.
